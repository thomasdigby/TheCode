WELCOME

The Code opened its doors back in September 2012 as a private member’s space designed with close friends & family in mind. We created an environment whose design is based around one’s audiovisual experience rather than plush interiors.

The club is located above the legendary Chinawhite, on Winsley Street, Fitzrovia. It currently houses ROXX, a late night pop-up bar & club concept re-creating the revelry & debauchery usually only found behind closed doors backstage. Our passion lies in electronic music and our goal is to share that passion with as many likeminded people as possible; we call them Codependents! The ritual of people coming together to celebrate a movement is the driving force behind The Code.

We regularly showcase leading House & Techno acts at secret locations in and around London. Every location is transformed to provide a unique party experience time after time. Discerning Codependents will find a true VIP table service and offerings tailored to address their individual needs.
